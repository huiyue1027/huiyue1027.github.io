Design Rationale & Features

The chosen theme for this assignment is a Music Video player. The main idea was to create a clean, distraction-free layout similar to popular streaming sites. By combining a simple background with a dark control bar, the visual focus stays completely on the video itself rather than the website around it.

Interactive Features

The player comes with a few custom interactive features to make it feel more natural to use. Instead of just clicking to skip around, the progress bar allows for smooth dragging to find a specific part of the video. Also, to save screen space and keep things looking neat, the volume slider is hidden at first. It only slides out when the mouse moves over the speaker icon.

For better visual feedback, the play and mute buttons swap their icons when clicked, making it clear what state the video is in. Finally, there is a fullscreen button to expand the view, along with a settings gear that opens up a quality menu, giving the player a much more complete and realistic feel.
