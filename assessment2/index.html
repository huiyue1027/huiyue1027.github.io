Design Rationale & Features

Context: For this Interactive Media assignment, I chose the "Music Video" (MV) context. My goal was to design a highly immersive, distraction-free environment that mimics professional streaming platforms like YouTube. I maintained a minimalist white/grey background with a dark, glass-like control bar to ensure the visual focus remains entirely on the video art.

Interactive Features (How to use):

  • Drag-to-Scrub Progress Bar: Unlike basic players, you can click or smoothly drag the red progress bar to seek through the video precisely.
  • Smart Volume Control: To save screen space and maintain a clean UI, the volume slider is hidden by default. Simply hover over the speaker icon to reveal the adjustable slider.
  • Dynamic Icons: The play/pause and mute icons provide immediate visual feedback by changing states when clicked.
  • Immersive Mode: Click the fullscreen icon (bottom right) to expand the video, or the settings gear to simulate quality adjustments (1080p/720p).
